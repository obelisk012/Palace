This is a card game called Palace

Rules:
- Keep playing higher than the current top card of the discard pile
- If you can't, you have to pick up the whole discard pile and add it to your hand
- You have 3 cards face down and 3 cards on top of them
- Once the deck and your hand have run out, you play these cards
- You win by running out of cards
- Some cards have special abilities:
    7 - The next card is lower than a 7
    8 - Copies the previous card
    10 - Burns the whole pile
    2 - Resets the discard pile
    Joker - The next card must be of a certain selected suit (Not added yet)
